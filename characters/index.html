Characters

James Lemonsile

James is your favorite 7 year old lemon! He loves to play games and really enjoys spending time with his friends and family.


Amanda

Amanda is James's 9 year old sister, she is very bratty and annoying to James.


Gregory

Gregory is the father of James and Amanda. He loves playing golf and loves to do activities with his children. They love to fish together, and sometimes, they catch big fish!


Sydney

Sydney is the wife of Gregory, and the mother of James and Amanda. She loves both of her children dearly, and helps them in their daily activities.


Eggboi

Eggboi is half egg and half boy. He is the pet of the Lemonsiles family, and loves to eat cheeseburgers.


Ricardo

Ricardo is James's best friend, and is a very good friend to everyone. He loves to teach Hunter the joys of being nice to everyone.


Hunter

Hunter is the school bully, sometimes, he is not very nice to James, but he does have his nice moments. Overtime, he grows to enjoy spending time with him and they became friends!
